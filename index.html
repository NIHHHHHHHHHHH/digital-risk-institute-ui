Who We Are

Built where academia
meets industry.

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute purpose-built for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments — from financial services to critical national infrastructure.

Through our UK university partnership, we connect the depth of academic research with the immediacy of live industry projects. Every programme is shaped by practitioners, ensuring graduates are deployment-ready from day one.
